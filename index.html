Frequently Asked Questions
What is the goal of the hackathon? +
We aim to promote development oriented policies, that support productive activities, decent job creation , entrepreneurship, creativity and innovation etc. which aligns with UN sustainable goals. Substantially increase the number of youth and adults who have relevant skills for employment , jobs and entrepreneurship. To introduce opportunities that students usually lack, real life working skills, rapid problem solving skills, thinking in terms of product, engaging in a team event where we support and grow and as an exposure to the industry where they get to socialise with experts, take their advice , learn from them and build.
Who can participate? Are there any prerequisites or required skills? +
The hackathon is open to all students. Though prior knowledge in coding and hardware would be beneficial , teams would also require members who have great ideas and presentation skills. In case you are a newbie and want to experience a good hackathon, you are at the right place. hack'21 is the right place for you to start!
How much does it cost? +
The registration for the Hackathon is completely free of cost. So all those who are interested are welcome to be a part of .hack(); '21
How is the event hosted? +
Due to the pandemic and to ensure the safety of our participants , we have decided to host the hackathon online. All students from anywhere across the globe can participate.
Who is organising the hackathon? +
The hackathon is organized by IEEE MACE SB.
Are there prizes? +
Of course yes! Amazing prizes and cool swags awaits you at hack'21.
